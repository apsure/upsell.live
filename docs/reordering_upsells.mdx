Grab handles appear when you have two or more upsells, allowing you to change the ordering presented to the customer.

<Screenshot alt="Grab handles shown with upsells" src="multiple-upsells.png"
  caption="Grab handles on the left of each upsell" />

To re-order, use your mouse (or finger on touch-devices) to drag and drop the upsells from the grab handles.
